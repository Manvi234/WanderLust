Anthens

Sure, here's a 7-day itinerary for Athens, Greece:

Day 1: Arrival and Acropolis

- Morning: Arrive in Athens and check into your accommodation.
- Afternoon: Visit the iconic Acropolis and explore the ancient ruins, including the Parthenon, Erechtheion, and Propylaea.
- Evening: Enjoy a leisurely stroll around Plaka, the historic neighborhood beneath the Acropolis, known for its charming streets and traditional tavernas.

Day 2: Ancient Agora and Monastiraki

- Morning: Explore the Ancient Agora, the heart of ancient Athens, and visit the Temple of Hephaestus.
- Afternoon: Wander through Monastiraki flea market, shop for souvenirs, and enjoy lunch at a local eatery.
- Evening: Take a relaxing sunset walk up Filopappou Hill for panoramic views of the city and the Acropolis.

Day 3: Day Trip to Delphi

- Join a guided day trip to Delphi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and ancient sanctuary. Explore the Temple of Apollo, the Delphi Archaeological Museum, and the stunning landscape.

Day 4: National Archaeological Museum and Lycabettus Hill

- Morning: Visit the National Archaeological Museum to see an incredible collection of artifacts from ancient Greece.
- Afternoon: Take a leisurely stroll through the upscale Kolonaki district and head to Lycabettus Hill for panoramic views and a beautiful sunset.
- Evening: Explore the Exarchia neighborhood known for its alternative and artistic vibe.

Day 5: Athens Riviera

- Morning: Head to the Athens Riviera and relax on one of the beautiful beaches like Vouliagmeni or Glyfada.
- Afternoon: Enjoy lunch at a seaside taverna and take a walk along the coastal promenade.
- Evening: Return to the city and explore the Gazi district for its vibrant nightlife.

Day 6: Piraeus and Sounion

- Morning: Take a short trip to Piraeus, the port of Athens, and explore the Mikrolimano marina.
- Afternoon: Continue to Cape Sounion to visit the Temple of Poseidon and enjoy breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea.
- Evening: Have a relaxing dinner by the sea in a traditional fish taverna.

Day 7: Museums and Farewell

- Morning: Visit the Benaki Museum to explore Greek art and history. - Afternoon: Explore the Byzantine and Christian Museum or the Museum of Cycladic Art.
- Evening: Enjoy a farewell dinner at a rooftop restaurant with views of the illuminated Acropolis.


Remember that this itinerary is just a suggestion, and you can adjust it based on your interests and the opening hours of attractions. Also, make sure to check for any changes in opening hours or restrictions due to unforeseen circumstances, especially considering the current global situation.
