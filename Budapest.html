Budapest

Certainly, here's a suggested 7-day itinerary for Budapest, Hungary. This itinerary will give you a well-rounded experience of the city's culture, history, architecture, and local flavors. Feel free to adjust the days according to your preferences and the operating hours of attractions.

Day 1: Arrival and Buda Castle District

- Morning: Arrival in Budapest, check-in to your accommodation.
- Afternoon: Explore the Buda Castle District. Visit Buda Castle, Fisherman's Bastion, and Matthias Church. Enjoy panoramic views of the city from the Castle Hill.
- Evening: Have dinner at a traditional Hungarian restaurant.

Day 2: Pest Side Exploration

- Morning: Visit the Hungarian Parliament Building and take a guided tour (book in advance).
- Afternoon: Stroll along Andrássy Avenue, visit the Hungarian State Opera House, and explore the Heroes' Square.
- Evening: Relax at the Széchenyi Thermal Bath, one of Budapest's famous thermal baths.

Day 3: Jewish Quarter and Ruin Bars

- Morning: Visit the Dohány Street Synagogue and the Jewish Museum.
- Afternoon: Explore the quirky ruin bars of the Jewish Quarter.
- Evening: Experience Budapest's vibrant nightlife in the Jewish Quarter with its lively bars and entertainment venues.

Day 4: Day Trip to Szentendre

- Take a day trip to the charming town of Szentendre, known for its artists' community, colorful houses, and galleries.

Day 5: Gellért Hill and Danube Cruise

- Morning: Hike up Gellért Hill for panoramic views of the city.
- Afternoon: Enjoy a leisurely stroll along the Danube Promenade, and consider taking a Danube River cruise to see the cityscape from the water.
- Evening: Explore the Váci Street for shopping and dining.

Day 6: Central Market Hall and City Park

- Morning: Visit the Great Market Hall for a taste of Hungarian cuisine and local products.
- Afternoon: Explore City Park (Városliget), visit Vajdahunyad Castle, and take a relaxing stroll around the park's lake.
- Evening: Try out a traditional Hungarian dinner.

Day 7: Margaret Island and Farewell

- Morning: Spend time on Margaret Island, a green oasis in the middle of the Danube. Enjoy its parks, gardens, and recreational facilities.
- Afternoon: Depending on your interests, you can visit museums like the Museum of Fine Arts or the Hungarian National Museum.
- Evening: Wrap up your trip with a farewell dinner and perhaps a nighttime walk along the Danube to see the illuminated buildings. Remember that this itinerary is just a suggestion, and you can adjust it based on your interests and the operating hours of attractions. Budapest has a lot to offer, from historical sites to contemporary culture, so tailor your trip to match your preferences. Enjoy your time in this beautiful city!
