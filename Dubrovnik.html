Dubrovnik

Certainly, here's a 7-day itinerary for exploring Dubrovnik:

Day 1: Arrival and Old Town Exploration

Arrive in Dubrovnik and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring the charming Old Town, walking through its historic streets and visiting landmarks like the city walls and the Rector's Palace.

Day 2: City Walls and Fort Lovrijenac

Start your day by walking the famous city walls for panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea and the city. Afterward, visit Fort Lovrijenac, known as the "Gibraltar of Dubrovnik," for more stunning views.

Day 3: Island-Hopping

Take a day trip to the nearby islands. Visit Lokrum Island for its botanical gardens and historic monastery, and then explore the Elafiti Islands for their natural beauty and beaches.

Day 4: Dubrovnik Cable Car and Sunset

Ride the Dubrovnik Cable Car to Mount SrÄ‘ for breathtaking views of the city and the surrounding area. Enjoy the sunset from the mountain and then return to the Old Town for a lovely evening.

Day 5: Game of Thrones Tour

If you're a fan of the TV series "Game of Thrones," take a guided tour to visit filming locations in and around Dubrovnik. Explore iconic sites like King's Landing and the Red Keep.

Day 6: Lokrum and Sea Kayaking

Spend another day on Lokrum Island, relaxing on its beaches and enjoying the natural beauty. In the afternoon, go sea kayaking along the Dubrovnik coastline for a unique perspective of the city.

Day 7: Day at Leisure and Departure

Use your last day to explore any remaining sites or simply relax by the sea. Depart from Dubrovnik with wonderful memories of your trip.
