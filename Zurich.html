Zurich

Sure, here's a 7-day itinerary for Zurich, Switzerland:

Day 1: Arrival and Old Town Exploration

- Arrive in Zurich and check into your hotel.
- Start your exploration with a walk through Zurich's Old Town (Altstadt). Visit Grossmünster and Fraumünster, two iconic churches.
- Stroll along the Bahnhofstrasse, one of the world's most exclusive shopping streets.
- Enjoy Swiss cuisine at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Lake Zurich and Museums

- Have breakfast and then take a boat cruise on Lake Zurich to enjoy the stunning views of the city and the surrounding Alps.
- Visit the Swiss National Museum to learn about Switzerland's cultural history.
- Explore the Kunsthaus Zurich, an impressive art museum featuring works by Swiss and international artists.

Day 3: Day Trip to Lucerne

- Take a train to Lucerne, a charming Swiss town known for its medieval architecture.
- Walk across the famous Kapellbrücke (Chapel Bridge) and visit the Swiss Transport Museum.
- Enjoy a leisurely boat ride on Lake Lucerne or take the cogwheel train to Mount Pilatus for panoramic views.

Day 4: Uetliberg Mountain and Parks

- Start your day with a hike or a train ride to Uetliberg Mountain for panoramic views of Zurich and the surrounding areas.
- After your hike, relax in one of Zurich's beautiful parks like Lindenhof Park or Botanical Garden.
- In the evening, explore Zurich West, an artsy district with trendy shops, galleries, and restaurants.

Day 5: Rhine Falls Day Trip

- Embark on a day trip to Rhine Falls, Europe's largest waterfall, located about an hour from Zurich.
- Take a boat ride to get up close to the falls or explore the trails around the area.
- Return to Zurich in the evening and unwind.

Day 6: Zurich Zoo and Chocolate Experience

- Spend your morning at Zurich Zoo, home to a diverse range of animals and habitats.
- In the afternoon, visit the Lindt Home of Chocolate to learn about Swiss chocolate history and indulge in some delicious treats.
- Take a relaxing evening walk along Lake Zurich's promenade.

Day 7: Day Trip to Bern

- Take a train to Bern, the capital city of Switzerland.
- Explore the UNESCO-listed Old Town, visit the Zytglogge clock tower, and see the Federal Palace.
- Return to Zurich in the evening for your last night in the city.

Feel free to adjust this itinerary based on your interests and the time of year you're visiting. Also, remember to check the opening hours and any travel restrictions due to the current situation before you go.
