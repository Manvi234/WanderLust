Santorini

Certainly, here's a 7-day itinerary for exploring Santorini:

Day 1: Arrival and Sunset in Oia

Arrive in Santorini and check into your hotel. Explore the charming village of Oia and enjoy a breathtaking sunset view.

Day 2: Fira and Caldera Views

Visit Fira, the capital of Santorini. Explore the narrow streets, enjoy cafes, and take in the stunning caldera views. Don't miss the Archaeological Museum.

Day 3: Beach Day at Perissa

Spend a relaxing day at Perissa Beach. Enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and beachfront restaurants.

Day 4: Akrotiri Archaeological Site

Explore the ancient ruins of Akrotiri, a well-preserved Minoan settlement buried by a volcanic eruption.

Day 5: Winery Tour

Discover Santorini's unique vineyards and taste local wines. Visit wineries in areas like Megalochori and enjoy the sunset from Santo Wines.

Day 6: Boat Tour to Volcano and Hot Springs

Take a boat tour to the volcano, hike to its crater, and swim in the hot springs. Enjoy a BBQ on board and admire the island from the sea.

Day 7: Kamari and Departure

Visit Kamari Beach for your last day. Relax, shop for souvenirs, and savor local cuisine. Depart from Santorini with beautiful memories.
