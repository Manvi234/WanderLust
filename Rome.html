Rome

Certainly, here's a suggested 7-day itinerary for your trip to Rome:

Day 1: Arrival and Ancient Rome Exploration

- Morning: Arrive in Rome, check-in to your accommodation.
- Afternoon: Start with the Colosseum and Roman Forum. Explore these iconic ancient ruins.
- Evening: Wander around the Palatine Hill area and enjoy a traditional Italian dinner in the nearby Trastevere district.

Day 2: Vatican City and Castel Sant'Angelo

- Morning: Visit the Vatican City. Explore St. Peter's Basilica and climb to the top of St. Peter's Dome for panoramic views.
- Afternoon: Explore the Vatican Museums, including the Sistine Chapel.
- Evening: Visit Castel Sant'Angelo, an ancient fortress with stunning views of the city.

Day 3: Baroque Rome

- Morning: Explore the Spanish Steps and the Trevi Fountain. Take some time for shopping along Via del Corso.
- Afternoon: Visit the Pantheon, a marvel of ancient Roman engineering.
- Evening: Enjoy an evening stroll through Piazza Navona and its surrounding streets.

Day 4: Day Trip to Tivoli

- Take a day trip to Tivoli, a nearby town known for Villa d'Este's stunning gardens and Hadrian's Villa, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Day 5: Modern and Artistic Rome

- Morning: Visit the MAXXI Museum of 21st Century Arts for a dose of modern art and architecture.
- Afternoon: Explore the Galleria Borghese, home to an impressive collection of sculptures and paintings.
- Evening: Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of the Piazza del Popolo.

Day 6: Appian Way and Catacombs

- Morning: Explore the ancient Appian Way, lined with historic ruins and catacombs.
- Afternoon: Visit the Catacombs of San Callisto or San Sebastiano for an intriguing underground experience.
- Evening: Relax with a leisurely dinner and soak in the ambiance of the city.

Day 7: Leisure and Farewell

- Morning: Spend some relaxed time exploring the charming streets, perhaps revisiting your favorite spots.
- Afternoon: Visit Villa Borghese park for a peaceful stroll or a boat ride on the lake.
- Evening: Enjoy a farewell dinner at a local trattoria, reflecting on your wonderful time in Rome.

Remember that this itinerary is just a suggestion and can be customized based on your interests and pace. Additionally, make sure to check the opening hours of attractions, book tickets in advance where necessary, and consider local events or holidays that might impact your plans. Enjoy your time in Rome!
