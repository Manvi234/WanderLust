Prague

Certainly, here's a 7-day itinerary for exploring Prague:

Day 1: Arrival and Old Town

- Arrive in Prague and check into your hotel.

- Explore Prague's Old Town Square, visit the Astronomical Clock.

- Enjoy traditional Czech dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Prague Castle and Charles Bridge

- Visit Prague Castle, including St. Vitus Cathedral and Golden Lane.

- Walk along the historic Charles Bridge, enjoy the views.

- Explore Malá Strana district in the afternoon.

Day 3: Jewish Quarter and Vltava River

- Discover the Jewish Quarter, visit the Jewish Museum.

- Take a leisurely cruise on the Vltava River.

- Enjoy Czech cuisine at a riverside restaurant.

Day 4: Day Trip to Kutná Hora

- Take a day trip to the medieval town of Kutná Hora.

- Visit the Sedlec Ossuary (Bone Church) and St. Barbara's Church.

Day 5: Explore Lesser Town and Petřín Hill

- Explore Lesser Town's charming streets and squares.

- Climb Petřín Hill for panoramic views of Prague.

- Relax in Kampa Park by the riverside.

Day 6: National Museum and Wenceslas Square

- Visit the National Museum and learn about Czech history.

- Walk along bustling Wenceslas Square, shop and dine.

Day 7: Local Markets and Farewell

- Explore local markets like Havelské Tržiště.

- Spend your last hours shopping for souvenirs.

- Bid farewell to Prague and depart.
