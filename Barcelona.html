Barcelona

Certainly, here's a 7-day itinerary for exploring Barcelona:

Day 1: Arrival and Gothic Quarter

Arrive in Barcelona and check into your hotel. Explore the historic Gothic Quarter with its narrow streets and visit the Barcelona Cathedral.

Day 2: La Sagrada Familia and Park Güell

Visit the iconic La Sagrada Familia, Gaudí's masterpiece. In the afternoon, explore the colorful Park Güell with its mosaic-covered architecture.

Day 3: Montjuïc Hill and Magic Fountain

Take a cable car or hike up Montjuïc Hill for panoramic views. Visit Montjuïc Castle and enjoy the evening light show at the Magic Fountain.

Day 4: Beach Day and Barri Gòtic

Spend a relaxing day at Barceloneta Beach. In the afternoon, explore the Barri Gòtic further, and visit Picasso Museum.

Day 5: Day Trip to Montserrat

Take a day trip to Montserrat, a stunning mountain monastery. Explore the basilica and enjoy the beautiful hiking trails.

Day 6: Passeig de Gràcia and Casa Batlló

Stroll down Passeig de Gràcia, known for its luxury shops. Visit Casa Batlló, another architectural gem by Gaudí.

Day 7: Park de la Ciutadella and Farewell

Explore Park de la Ciutadella, go rowing on the lake, and visit the Barcelona Zoo. Spend your last evening enjoying tapas and reflecting on your trip.

Remember to check for any specific opening hours and travel advisories before your trip.
