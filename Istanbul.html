Istanbul

Certainly, here's a 7-day itinerary for exploring Istanbul, a city rich in history, culture, and stunning architecture.

Day 1: Arrival and Sultanahmet

- Morning: Arrive in Istanbul and check into your accommodation in the Sultanahmet area, which is close to many historical attractions.
- Afternoon: Visit the Hagia Sophia, an iconic architectural marvel that was originally a cathedral and later transformed into a mosque and museum.
- Evening: Explore the Blue Mosque (Sultan Ahmed Mosque) known for its striking blue tiles and intricate design. Take a leisurely stroll through the Hippodrome Square and enjoy the views of the surrounding area.

Day 2: Topkapi Palace and Grand Bazaar

- Morning: Begin your day at Topkapi Palace, once the residence of Ottoman sultans. Explore its beautiful courtyards, pavilions, and treasury.
- Afternoon: Head to the Grand Bazaar, one of the world's oldest and largest covered markets. Shop for traditional Turkish goods, from carpets to spices.
- Evening: Enjoy a Bosphorus cruise to witness the city's skyline and the beauty of the strait that separates Europe and Asia.

Day 3: Bosphorus and Beyoglu

- Morning: Visit the Dolmabah√ße Palace, an opulent 19th-century palace with stunning interiors and impressive gardens.
- Afternoon: Explore the vibrant Beyoglu district. Walk down Istiklal Avenue, a bustling pedestrian street lined with shops, cafes, and galleries.
- Evening: Reach Galata Tower before sunset for panoramic views of Istanbul. Then, dine at a local restaurant in Beyoglu.

Day 4: Chora Church and Spice Bazaar

- Morning: Visit the Chora Church (Kariye Museum) known for its exquisite Byzantine mosaics and frescoes.
- Afternoon: Head to the Spice Bazaar (Egyptian Bazaar) to immerse yourself in a world of aromatic spices, sweets, and other local products.
- Evening: Relax with a traditional Turkish bath experience at a historic hammam.

Day 5: Asian Side and Kadikoy

- Morning: Take a ferry to the Asian side of Istanbul. Explore Kadikoy, known for its vibrant markets, cafes, and diverse food scene.
- Afternoon: Visit the Beylerbeyi Palace, another elegant Ottoman residence located on the Asian shore.
- Evening: Enjoy a leisurely stroll along the Kadikoy coastline and savor dinner at a seafood restaurant.

Day 6: Princes' Islands

- Morning: Take a ferry to the Princes' Islands, a group of picturesque islands in the Sea of Marmara. Explore the serene atmosphere, rent bicycles, and enjoy the absence of motorized vehicles.
- Afternoon: Enjoy a horse-drawn carriage ride around the island and indulge in a seafood lunch.
- Evening: Return to Istanbul and relax or explore any missed spots.

Day 7: Modern Istanbul and Farewell

- Morning: Visit the modern districts of Nisantasi or Sisli for shopping at upscale boutiques and shopping centers.
- Afternoon: Explore the Istanbul Modern Art Museum to experience contemporary Turkish art.
- Evening: Have a farewell dinner at a rooftop restaurant, soaking in the last views of the city.

Remember that this itinerary is just a suggestion, and you can adjust it based on your interests and the operating hours of attractions. Also, make sure to check for any COVID-19 related travel restrictions or changes to opening hours before your trip.
