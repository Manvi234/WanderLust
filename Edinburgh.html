Edinburgh

Certainly, here's a 7-day itinerary for exploring Edinburgh:

Day 1: Arrival and Introduction

Arrive in Edinburgh and check into your hotel. Take a leisurely stroll down the Royal Mile to get a feel for the historic city center. Enjoy a traditional Scottish dinner at a local pub.

Day 2: Edinburgh Castle and Old Town

Visit the iconic Edinburgh Castle, perched atop Castle Rock. Explore the castle's history and enjoy panoramic views of the city. Wander through the charming Old Town, and don't miss the Camera Obscura and World of Illusions.

Day 3: Arthur's Seat and Holyrood Palace

Hike up Arthur's Seat, an extinct volcano, for breathtaking views of Edinburgh. In the afternoon, explore the historic Holyrood Palace, the official residence of the monarch in Scotland.

Day 4: Royal Yacht Britannia and Leith

Visit the Royal Yacht Britannia, the former royal yacht of Queen Elizabeth II. Explore the vibrant port district of Leith, known for its restaurants and bars.

Day 5: National Museum of Scotland and Princes Street Gardens

Discover the exhibits at the National Museum of Scotland, covering a wide range of topics. Take a stroll through Princes Street Gardens, located below the impressive Edinburgh Castle.

Day 6: Day Trip to Scottish Highlands

Embark on a guided day trip to the Scottish Highlands. Explore the stunning landscapes, visit Loch Ness, and experience the natural beauty of Scotland.

Day 7: Royal Mile Shops and Departure

Spend your last day shopping for souvenirs along the Royal Mile. Enjoy your final meal in Edinburgh before departing for your next destination.
