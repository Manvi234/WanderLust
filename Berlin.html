Berlin

Certainly, here's a 7-day itinerary for exploring Berlin:

Day 1: Arrival and Introduction to the City

- Morning: Arrive in Berlin and check into your accommodation.
- Afternoon: Start your trip with a visit to the iconic Brandenburg Gate and explore the Unter den Linden boulevard.
- Evening: Head to Alexanderplatz to see the famous TV Tower. Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Historical Berlin

- Morning: Visit the Berlin Wall Memorial and Checkpoint Charlie to learn about the city's divided history.
- Afternoon: Explore the Topography of Terror exhibition and the Holocaust Memorial.
- Evening: Take a leisurely stroll along the Spree River and enjoy dinner at a riverside restaurant.

Day 3: Museum Island and Culture

- Morning: Spend your morning at Museum Island, home to several world-renowned museums like the Pergamon Museum and Neues Museum.
- Afternoon: Visit the Berlin Cathedral and enjoy a walk through Lustgarten Park.
- Evening: Attend a classical music concert or theatrical performance at one of Berlin's cultural venues.

Day 4: Modern Berlin

- Morning: Explore the East Side Gallery, a long stretch of the Berlin Wall covered in murals.
- Afternoon: Visit the East Side Mall for shopping and lunch.
- Evening: Discover the vibrant Kreuzberg district, known for its street art, diverse cuisine, and nightlife.

Day 5: Day Trip to Potsdam

- Morning: Take a train to Potsdam, a picturesque city known for Sanssouci Palace and its beautiful gardens.
- Afternoon: Explore Sanssouci Park, visit the palace, and enjoy the charming streets of Potsdam's historic center.
- Evening: Return to Berlin and relax or explore a local neighborhood.

Day 6: Green Berlin

- Morning: Start your day at Tiergarten, Berlin's central park. Rent a bike or enjoy a leisurely walk.
- Afternoon: Visit the Berlin Zoo, located within Tiergarten.
- Evening: Have a picnic in Tiergarten or dine at a nearby restaurant.
